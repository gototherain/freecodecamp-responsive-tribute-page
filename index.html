David Bowie’s Career Milestones

  • 8, 1947 David Bowie is born in London.
  • 1969 The breakthrough single “Space Oddity,” is released.
  • 1972 Plays his first New York show.
  • 1976 Stars in the movie “The Man Who Fell To Earth.”
  • 1981 Collaborates with the band Queen on the single “Under Pressure.”
  • 1985 Covers “Dancing in the Street” with Mick Jagger to raise money for the famine-relief charity Live Aid.
  • 1989 His newly formed band, Tin Machine, releases its first album.
  • 2000 Performs a headliner act at the Glastonbury Festival.
  • 2006 Gives his final public concert, a three-song set at a charity fund-raiser in New York.
  • 2015 “Lazarus,” a musical featuring new music by Mr. Bowie, opens off Broadway at the New York Theater Workshop
  • 2016 “Blackstar,” his final album, is released days before his death.
